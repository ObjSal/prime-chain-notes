Chain Notes — Companion static

The online half of the offline notes app: builds sync bundles from the blockchain for the device to import, and broadcasts the transactions the device signs. No keys ever touch this page.

Network

1 · Build a sync bundle

—balance (sats)
—utxos
—note-carrying txs
—chain height
—fees sat/vB (f/h/e)

2 · Broadcast signed transactions

Drop the .hex file(s) the device exported to /chain-notes/outbox, or paste raw hex.

Point the camera at the tx QR on the device's note screen ("Show tx QR"). Broadcasts automatically on scan.

Endpoints: mempool.space (mainnet/testnet4/signet) or the local regtest shim. Bundles contain only public chain data; transactions you broadcast are already signed and final.
